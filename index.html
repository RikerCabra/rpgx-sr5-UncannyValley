                      [Description]
                      Standing at 1.6m and weighing just around 49kg, Sabrina's build belies the malnutrition and difficulty of her childhood. Her blonde hair frequently features varying highlight colors and is kept up out of her face at most times. Her style is best described as subdued casual, mostly dressing to blend into the background of any location.

                      [Background]
                      Sabrina never knew her parents; her memories of early childhood are a slideshow of constantly changing foster homes and never understanding why she was unable to find a permanent home. She always assumed it was due in no small part to the trouble she got into whenever Sabrina got her hands on Matrix tech, even as a young child. She couldn’t help it, something tugged at her to continue delving in and learning more.

                      Fed up with the system, Sabrina ran away in her early teens, choosing a life in the shadows of Seattle proper over the constant cycle of disappointment. It was rough, but ultimately proved the better decision. She was able to find a waitress job for the first few years at a cyber café, giving her easy access to tech and a small source of income. Gradually, her career path shifted to match her life, taking a turn into the shadows providing matrix support for runners.

                      In the years since, she has made a name for herself as a freelance decker, outshining most of her so-called “peers” in raw talent and unique perspective. Despite her rough life, Sabrina has been able to carve out a good life for herself in Seattle. While she certainly isn’t the richest or most powerful decker around, her reputation is solid, the income enough to cover her needs and desires, and the risk minimal enough that she is not constantly on the lookout for threats.
